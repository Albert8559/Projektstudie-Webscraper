EP full-text data

Image

 

A bulk data collection including the full text in machine-readable format of all patent applications and granted patent specifications published by the EPO since it was set up in 1978.

Download a sample file

Download

This bulk data set is available in XML and PDF/A as frontfile and backfile data.

With EP full-text data you can:

  • access the full text of the entire collection of EP publications (A and B)
  • download weekly frontfiles
  • search the complete EP full-text collection using your own search interface, in-house database or search engine
Getting started
  1. Check the information on the main features of our bulk data sets.
  2. Download the weekly exchange files (available every Wednesday at 14.00 hrs CET).
Conditions
 Coverage
  • EP-A documents from 1978 onwards
  • EP-B documents from 1980 onwards

European patent applications (EP-A documents) filed via the PCT route and published by WIPO in one of the three official languages of the EPO are not republished by the EPO. For these cases, only minimum information is available (EP publication, corresponding PCT application number, etc.)

 FormatXML ST36 and PDF/A 
 VolumeFrontfile: approx. 4 GB (zipped)
Backfile: approx. 3.53 TB (zipped)
 DeliveryCurrent data (frontfile) is available as a weekly download.
Publication takes place every Wednesday, at 14.00 hrs CET.
Backfile data is available for download.

The EPO's bulk data sets are also referred to as "raw data products" or "EPO databases". 

The terms and conditions for licensing of EPO databases apply.

Further information

The EPO has a legal obligation to publish the patent applications it receives (EP-A documents) and the patents it grants (EP-B documents).

A weekly file containing all EP-A and EP-B documents for that week is published every Wednesday at 14.00 hrs.
